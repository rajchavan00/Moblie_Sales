📱 Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard
## 1. Project Title / Headline

📊 Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard: Smart Insights into Smartphone Sales Performance
A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, and brand performance across multiple cities and time periods.

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The Mobile Sales Analytics Dashboard is a visually rich and analytical Power BI report developed to monitor and evaluate smartphone sales performance across different brands, cities, payment methods, and mobile models.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of critical business KPIs such as:

Total Sales Revenue
Total Quantity Sold
Average Selling Price
Transaction Volume
Customer Ratings
Payment Method Trends
Brand-wise and Model-wise Performance

This solution is ideal for:

Retail Sales Analysts
Mobile Store Owners
Business Intelligence Teams
Marketing Strategists
Inventory Managers
Regional Sales Managers

The dashboard helps stakeholders identify high-performing products, customer preferences, and regional sales opportunities for better business decisions.

## 5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem

The smartphone retail industry operates in a highly competitive and rapidly changing environment where businesses need quick access to sales insights to optimize inventory, pricing, and marketing strategies.

However, raw transactional data makes it difficult to answer key business questions such as:

Which mobile brands generate the highest revenue?
Which cities contribute the most sales?
What payment methods are most preferred by customers?
Which months experience peak sales activity?
Which mobile models are top-performing?
How do customer ratings influence product performance?

Without a centralized analytical solution, identifying trends and opportunities becomes time-consuming and inefficient.

Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

Provide a complete overview of mobile sales performance.
Enable users to analyze sales trends across cities and time periods.
Identify top-performing mobile brands and models.
Monitor customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences.
Support inventory planning and strategic business decisions.
Deliver interactive and easy-to-understand visual insights.
